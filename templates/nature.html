China's Stone Forest is 270 million years old. As the name implies, this is basically a 150-square mile forest made of stone. Waitomo Glowworm Caves in New Zealand is home to thousands of twinkling glow worms. The caves have been open to the public since 1889, but known to the indigenous Maori people for much longer. The magical experience is heightened by the fact that visitors silently glide through the caves by gondola.
